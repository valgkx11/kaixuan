Assignment: Accessories Box

  • I am supposed to create an accessories box with either movable lid or door and drawers specifications as follows:
    • Must have a movable lid or door or hinges
    • Must have drawers
    • Must be lasercut (wood 2.5~3.6mm)
    • Drawn and modelled in Fusion 360
    • Can be glued only on the joints
    • Must be “decorated” in terms of design aesthetic

How I implement and create?

  • Using similar steps in creating the laptop stand, I created this paramatric laser cut box. Firstly, I started with a fusion 360 to sketch up my box.
    • I set my parameters that I want for my box like length, height and thickness at first it was easy however during the cutting it dosen't work that I want and I will go on with that later.
    • I started skecthing outer body of the box using hinges and using joineries that my lecturer have taught me in the lesson using a normal box.
    • I went on being creative by choosing some design for my box and create it in inkscape & Fusion 360.

    • How I put in drawers and hinges?

  • After that I went on to create hinges and that I learn from youtube. Not only that I created the drawer as well by used extruding and combine function in Fusion 360
    • I utilize the function of combine and extruding.
    • I put the divider to secure the drawer better.
    • I put drawer as well
    • I uses what I learn in the previous to innovate and create the final product to send for cutting.

    How I put into laser cutting?

  • I save my sketch as dxf in Fusion 360 and transfer to inkscape to check whether all the skecth is in a good condition before going to the lab and cut
  • And also engrave with designs.
    • In T11C, not all the board is the dimension we want so we have to plus minus the thickness to give to the joineries be more clean than uneven joineries.
    • Setting parameters is important so that with having the different thickness of the plywood we will be able to change in the parameters setting.
    • I feel that overall the laser cutting experience was mindblowing and fun for me. For me, I would prefer laser cutting than 3d printing as it is faster. I found out that both T11C(LC-EpilogPro-11) and T1442(LC-Universal660-14) have different way of cutting in the laser by a few mm away and I feel T11C is more accurate and faster.
    • However, it might look like is nice from the outside but inside I glued extra layered of wood to support the drawer. As I didn't manage to finish on time.
