Assignment: Accessories Box

  • I am supposed to create an accessories box with either movable lid or door and drawers specifications as follows:
    • Must have a movable lid or door
    • Must have drawers
    • Must be lasercut (wood 2.5~3.6mm)
    • Drawn and modelled in Fusion 360
    • Can be glued only on the joints
    • No nails, hinges etc
    • Must be “decorated” in terms of design aesthetic
