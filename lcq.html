Laser Cutting Introduction

  • Laser cutting is a manufacturing process that uses a high-powered laser beam to cut or engrave materials.
  • The laser beam is focused using lenses or mirrors and directed onto the material to be cut, where it heats up and vaporizes the material, creating a cut or an engraving.
  • The material being cut can vary from:
    • Metal
    • Wood
    • Plastics
    • Fabrics
    • Leather and etc.

Laser Cutting Quiz

  • Before we can do Laser Cutting:
    • We have to go book online lesson in flms and do it online in brightspace.(same as 3D printing)
    • We have to do the quiz after the lesson and score 80 and above then one slight difference is that we have to go through a practical demonstration that I have did during my main module in my course to test whether we know how to used and after passing we will be able to utilize the Laser Cutting Machine.
    • After that points will be given to us to allow us to utilize the Laser Cutting Machine so we have to book in flms before going there.

Practical Demonstration

  • We have to do it from the computer at T1442(for my test):


    • We have to import the dxf file(that is given by the lecturer there)and press OK


    • We have to click the bottom fill box button whether to engrave or cut using RGB so red is cut and green is engrave and change to hairline.


    • Click File > Print > Cogwheel for settings > Load > Select the correct material and thickness and Click open > OK > PRINT


    • Click UCP > Place the material in the laser cutting machine at the top left > Click on the ON button > Used Focus tool to show where it start to cut > Click Moving Tools to move to the empty space to click to the pointer > click estimate time button > Shut the lid and press start.


    • Monitor the cut by glancing at it > Once done wait for around 1 minute before opening the lid > Tap the cut material make sure is fully cut through > Remove unwanted materials > deactivate the laser cutting and switch off the necessary switches > no need to shut down PC
